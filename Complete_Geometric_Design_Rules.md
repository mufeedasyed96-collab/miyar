- Road Geometric Design Manual (TR-514 / Second Edition - January 2022) – 794 pages
- Standard Drawing Guideline Part 1 (TR-541-1 / Third Edition - June 2024) – 72 pages

**Document Purpose:** This unified document combines all validation conditions from both TR-514 and TR-541-geo, organized by category for comprehensive geometric compliance checking for Roads, Bridges, and Tunnels infrastructure projects.
