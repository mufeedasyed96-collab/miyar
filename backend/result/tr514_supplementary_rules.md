## Textual Evidence

### Section 7.4.4 Travelled Way and Shoulders (Page 352 / 340)
> "Through-traffic lanes should be 3.65 m wide."

### Section 7.4.8 Structures (Page 354 / 342)
> "The clear width on bridges carrying freeway traffic should be as wide as the approach roadway."

### Section 7.4.9 Vertical Clearance (Page 353 / 341)
> "The vertical clearance to roadway structures... should be at least **6.5 m**... Where the freeway passes over a railroad, provide a vertical clearance of **7.5 m**."

### Section 7.4.10 Roadside Design (Page 354 / 342)
> "Retaining walls and piers... preferably should be at least **0.6 m** beyond the outer edge of shoulder."
