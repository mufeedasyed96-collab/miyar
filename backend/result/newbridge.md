## Chapter 12: Grade Separations and Interchanges (Start Page: 588)

### Section 12.0: Vertical Clearances (Article B1)
**Reference Page:** 588 (Verified)
*   **B1.1 Highway Overhead:** Minimum **6.5m** vertical clearance for structures over highways.
*   **B1.2 Urban Overhead:** Minimum **6.0m** vertical clearance for structures in urban areas.
*   **B1.3 Signs/Ventilation:** Minimum **6.5m** to bottom of overhead signs/ventilation.
*   **B1.4 Railroad Overpass:** Minimum **7.5m** vertical clearance (Truck route standard).
*   **B1.5 Navigable Channel:** Minimum **8.5m** vertical clearance for bridges over navigable channels.
*   **B1.6 Camel Underpass:** Minimum **6.5m** vertical clearance in rural areas.
*   **B1.7 Temporary Detour:** Minimum **6.0m** vertical clearance during construction.

### Section 12.3: Bridge Roadway Width (Article B3)
**Reference Page:** 589 (Verified by User)
*   **B3.1 Approach Width:** Bridge roadway width must **match** the full approach roadway width (lanes + shoulders).
*   **B3.2 Railing Compatibility:** Bridge railing stiffness must transition compatible with roadside barrier.
*   **B3.3 Openings:** Bridge openings at grade separation should be maximized (eliminate piers where practical).

### Section 12.4: Horizontal Clearances (Article B2)
**Reference Page:** 610 (Verified by User)
*   **B2.1 Abutment Clearance:** Minimum **1.5m** from edge of roadway to bridge abutment.
*   **B2.2 Pier Clearance:** Minimum **0.6m** from edge of travel lane to pier/column.
*   **B2.3 Sign Post (>60 km/h):** Minimum **9.0m** clearance from edge of carriageway for sign posts.

### Section 12.5: Bridge Grades (Article B6)
**Reference Page:** 615 (Estimated - After Section 12.4)
*   **B6.1 Longitudinal Grade:** Minimum **0.5%** longitudinal grade for bridge deck (required for drainage).

## Chapter 11: Roadside Safety (Start Page: 546)

### Section 11.4: Barriers and Parapets (Article B4)
**Reference Page:** 558 (Verified by User)
*   **B4.1 Compliance:** Barriers must meet **MASH** or **NCHRP-350** standards (MASH preferred).
*   **B4.2 Concrete Barrier Height:** Standard heights are **810mm** or **1070mm**.
*   **B4.3 ZOI (Concrete):** Zone of Intrusion is **0.5m**.
*   **B4.4 ZOI (W-Beam):** Zone of Intrusion is **1.6m**.
*   **B4.5 ZOI (Wire Rope):** Zone of Intrusion is **2.0m**.
*   **B4.6 Sand Areas:** Open tubular type railing is preferred over concrete in sand areas to prevent accumulation.
